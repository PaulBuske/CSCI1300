Cool Facts About Spring, Texas

  • It was founded in 1836.
  • The Goodyear airship America was stationed here from 1969 to 1992
  • The population increased from 700 to 62,559 between 1947 and 2020 in a 23 sq mile area
  • Old Town Spring has had much of the same buildings since the 1870s
  • Bonnie and Clyde robbed a bank here in the 1930s
